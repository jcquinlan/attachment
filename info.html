.attachment|
a typology

.attachment is the manifestation of my curiosity of love letters. I felt that I needed a way to comfortably situate such allegedly meaningful documents within the scope of the uninvited. I was unsure how something written from the most vulnerable recesses of one's being would affect a third party, if at all. I also wanted to expose the letters for whatever it is they were. I spent so much time developing an air of distance and inaccessibility around the letters that I needed to undo my work, to lay each document out, exposed and arrested in time in an effort to gain control over the words.

This ongoing project is the antithesis of everything I had done before it.
