.attachment|
a typology

.attachment is a small collection of transcribed love letters curated in an effort to examine the expression of our thoughts and feelings. I was curious how written word would situate itself when removed from it's original context, how we attach meaning to what we say, and where that meaning manifests itself. Read here for more information.
